Cooking in Casablanca is your go-to website for easy, traditional and tasty Moroccan recipes. We add a new recipe each day, and have an ever-expanding library of tried and tested dishes for you to cook at home.

You can save the recipes which you want to try at a later date, and add your comments below each recipe to let us know what you think! You can also read helpful tips from our users in the comments section, and we’ll always try to answer the questions you leave so don’t be shy!

On the useful links page, you’ll find info on where to buy the best Moroccan essentials, and if you write to use on our contact page, we’ll ensure to get back to you within 48 hours.

We hope you like the website, and the Moroccan dishes you cook at home even more!
